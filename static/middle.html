Colonial Studies

The Middle Colonies

New York, Pennsylvania, New Jersey, Delaware

Economic Factors

  • They served the biggest role in the English mercantile system
  • Relied heavily on agriculture, predominately corn
  • Built ships and traded with the New England colonies

Social Factors

  • Multiple religions: Mennonites, Lutherans, Dutch Calvinists, Presbyterians
  • A small part of the population was made up of Quakers
  • Dominance of one religion was almost impossible

Political Factors

Environmental

  • Very fertile soil, making it great for farming
  • Wheat and corn were the primary crops grown in Pennsylvania
